How winners are picked

Votes matter, but they do not decide the final outcome. The DAEMON team uses voting as signal, then selects the three best entries to ship and spotlight.

Why it matters

Winning projects get real distribution: shipped features, public credit, pro access, and direct entry into the core builder community.
